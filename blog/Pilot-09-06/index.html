Pilot - Technology & Today

An introduction to the terrifyingly wonderful world of technology.

By: Eden Breatnach
Published: 09 June 2026

Modern technology is terrifying. From self-driving cars taking people from A to B, to the rise of AI. The world of technology is evolving at a rate we cannot even comprehend.

The world we live in is nothing like before. We have access to all the information we could want - yet some of us cannot use the brain power to write an essay. We are the most technologically advanced generation - yet this presents a monumental fear of unemployment.

Technology is amazing. I certainly love to code, and build circuits. I love to create electronic gifts or mechanical toys. I love to build things to develop my skills. However, I am aware that technology has myriad negatives. I believe we need to have an intelligent conversation to ensure the safety and security of future generations without getting rid of this marvel altogether.

If these are ideas that interest you - keep an eye on this blog. I discuss my own takes on both the faults and benefits of modern technology.
