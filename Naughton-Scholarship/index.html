The Naughton Scholarship

Apply here

In October of 2025, I had the absolute privilege of being awarded the Naughton Scholarship. This award honors excellence in science, technology, engineering and mathematics. I am unbelievably grateful for the freedom this award has given me on my academic journey.

The Naughton foundation offers exceptional support and unimaginable opportunities to their scholars.

Not only does the scholarship support you through a three to four year college degree financially - it offers the unexplainable experience of a weekend in a castle with your fellow scholars.

I have met phenomenal people thanks to this achievement. Anyone who is sitting the Leaving Certificate exams with the intention of studying in STEM on the island of Ireland must apply - this is a massive opportunity.
