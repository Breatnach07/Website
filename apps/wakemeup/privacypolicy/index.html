Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This policy outlines how data is handled within the WakeMeUp application. Your privacy is paramount, and the application is designed to collect minimal data necessary for functionality.

Data Collection and Storage

All configuration profiles, alarm sequences, and application settings are stored strictly locally on your physical device. No personal telemetry or biometric profiles are transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

If integrated with third-party libraries (such as system intent APIs or basic analytical tracking framework logs), data is handled in strict compliance with standard operating system sandbox rules.
